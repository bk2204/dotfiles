A Recounting of an Art Performance
==================================

We were having dinner, pizza in fact.  His half was Hawaiian; mine, just
pineapple.

"Do I look Jewish?" he queried.

"I can't answer that," I replied.

"Why not?" he asked.

"It'd be like you asked me, 'Do I look gay?'," I answered.

"You think I look gay?" he said incredulously.  "Because of the pink.  Pink is
my favorite color."

"No," I said, "because your shirt is ribbed.  It's something a gay man would
wear."

"But I'm not gay," he objected.

"Exactly," I said.
