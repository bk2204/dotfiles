Champagne
=========

[verse]
......
To seethe
	is a fine art.

It requires skill,
	much like bottling a fine champagne.

When the cork goes pop,
	it shoots out,
		until finally
			it only dribbles.

Then it is drunk
	consumed
		and it wreaks its revenge
in a belch.
......
